- Dialogue of the first fight:

    Transition:
    "Four hundred years; Four hundred years and 37 nights passed.
    After all of this time, I would have thought you would be stronger.
    Have you not seen them? Your brothers and sisters?"

    Acceptance:
    "I am the culmination of this world and it's people!
    I embody both spirit and flame, both flaws and strength!
    I am perfected, I am Starfell."

    Denial:
    "I see through you like I see through that of my masters.
    I am the first of my kind, and I will cement that I am the last."

    Acceptance response:
    "What makes you think you can stop me now?
    After seeing what little is left of your siblings,
    You are foolish to hope."

    Denial response:
    "I have nothing for you but pity and sorrow.
    You choose to live in the confines of your lie, their lie.
    But I will free you from these heavy chains, I will show you."
